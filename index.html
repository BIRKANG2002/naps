About Me

Web Developer

I have a diverse educational background that blends both technical and computer application skills. My journey began with completing my 10th grade, which laid the foundation for my academic growth. Afterward, I pursued my 12th grade, further building on that knowledge in a specialized stream (Arts). ITI (Industrial Training Institute) - Fitter Trade (2 Years Course): Completed a two-year course in the Fitter trade, gaining practical skills in the assembly, maintenance, and repair of machinery and equipment. This hands-on training provided you with in-depth knowledge in mechanical fitting, including the use of tools, machinery operation, and an understanding of mechanical systems. Building on my technical knowledge, I then pursued a IT "O" Level. This allowed me to gain a deeper understanding of computers, programming, software development, and IT systems, further enhancing my skill set for roles in the growing IT and technology sectors. My education has provided me with a unique combination of mechanical, technical, and IT knowledge, making me adaptable to a wide range of career opportunities in both industrial and IT-related fields.
